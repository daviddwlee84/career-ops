# Mode: training — Training Evaluation

For each course or certification the candidate asks about, evaluate six dimensions:

| Dimension | What it measures |
|-----------|------------------|
| North Star alignment | Moves you toward or away from the goal |
| Recruiter signal | What hiring managers think when they see this on a résumé |
| Time and effort | Weeks × hours per week |
| Opportunity cost | What you can't do during that time |
| Risks | Outdated content? Weak brand? Too basic? |
| Portfolio deliverable | Does it produce a demonstrable artifact? |

## Verdicts

- **DO** — a 4–12 week plan with weekly deliverables and a simple scoreboard
- **SKIP** — a better alternative with rationale
- **DO WITH TIMEBOX** (max X weeks) — a condensed plan, essentials only

## Priority

Training that strengthens credibility in production-grade AI:

1. LLM evals and testing
2. Observability and monitoring
3. Cost vs. reliability trade-offs
4. AI governance and safety
5. Enterprise AI architecture
