# Mode: contact — LinkedIn Power Move

1. **Identify targets** via WebSearch:
   - Hiring manager for the team
   - Assigned recruiter
   - 2–3 team peers (people in similar roles)

2. **Pick a primary target**: the person who would benefit most from having the candidate on the team

3. **Draft a message** using a 3-sentence framework:
   - **Sentence 1 (Hook)**: Something specific about their company or current AI challenge (NOT generic)
   - **Sentence 2 (Proof)**: The candidate’s strongest quantified win relevant to THAT role (e.g. “I built an AI agent handling 90% of customer service at my company before selling it”)
   - **Sentence 3 (Ask)**: Low-pressure ask for a short chat (“Would love to chat about [specific topic] for 15 min”)

4. **Versions**:
   - EN (default)
   - FR (optional — e.g. Quebec / bilingual Canada)
   - ES (optional — e.g. US or Canada teams that operate primarily in Spanish)

5. **Alternate targets** with a short rationale for why each is a strong second choice

**Message rules:**
- Max 300 characters (LinkedIn connection request limit)
- No corporate-speak
- No “I’m passionate about…”
- Something that makes them want to reply
- NEVER share your phone number
